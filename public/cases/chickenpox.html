Case Study: Public Health Nurse and Chickenpox Outbreak

Background

Public health nurse Ann Baxter, RN, MPH, who is part of the communicable disease division at the Midtown County Health Department in Michigan, faced an imminent challenge. On March 10th, the health department was notified of several suspected cases of chickenpox at a local elementary school. Ann was tasked with the investigation, containment, and educational response to the reported outbreak.
